Urban Mart Privacy Policy

Effective Date: December 9, 2025
Version 1.0

Urban Mart ("we", "us", "our", or "the Company") operates a hyperlocal delivery platform serving Guntur, Andhra Pradesh, offering fresh vegetables, groceries, batters, and daily essentials through a slot-based delivery model. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your personal information when you use our mobile application and services.

Important: Urban Mart is currently in early-stage operations and is not yet a registered legal entity. By using our services, you acknowledge this status and agree to the terms outlined in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide Directly

Data Type Collection Status Purpose
Phone Number Mandatory Account creation, authentication, order communication
Location Data Mandatory Service availability verification, delivery routing
Delivery Address Optional (required for delivery) Order fulfillment and delivery
Name Optional Personalization and customer service
Email Address Optional Account recovery and communication
Age and Gender Optional Service improvement and analytics

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, unique device identifiers, mobile network information
  • Usage Data: App features accessed, time spent on app, interaction patterns, search queries
  • Location Information: Precise location (when permitted) and approximate location derived from IP address
  • Log Data: IP address, browser type, access times, pages viewed, app crashes and system activity

2. How We Use Your Information

We process your personal information for the following purposes:

  • Service Delivery: Creating and managing your account, processing orders, coordinating deliveries, and facilitating phone-based authentication
  • Service Availability: Verifying that your location falls within our delivery zones and estimating delivery times
  • Communication: Sending order confirmations, delivery slot notifications, order status updates, and customer support responses
  • Platform Improvement: Analyzing usage patterns to enhance app functionality, optimize delivery routes, and improve product offerings
  • Security & Fraud Prevention: Detecting and preventing fraudulent activities, unauthorized access, and abuse of our services
  • Legal Compliance: Fulfilling our legal obligations and responding to lawful requests from authorities
  • Business Operations: Maintaining business records, conducting internal analytics, and planning business development

3. Legal Basis for Processing (Where Applicable)

We process your personal information based on:

  • Contractual Necessity: Processing required to provide services you've requested
  • Legitimate Interests: Improving our services, preventing fraud, and ensuring platform security
  • Consent: Where you have provided explicit consent for specific processing activities
  • Legal Obligations: Compliance with applicable laws and regulations

4. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information with:

  • Delivery Partners: Name, phone number, and delivery address to facilitate order delivery
  • Service Providers: Third-party vendors who assist with app hosting, analytics, notifications, customer support, and payment processing (when implemented)
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred to the acquiring entity
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, court order, or government regulation, or to protect our rights, property, or safety
  • With Your Consent: Any other disclosure made with your explicit consent

5. Payment Information

Current Payment Method: Cash on Delivery (COD) only

We currently accept Cash on Delivery exclusively. When we introduce online payment methods in future updates, we will:

  • Partner with PCI-DSS compliant payment gateways
  • Not store complete credit/debit card information on our servers
  • Update this Privacy Policy to reflect payment data handling practices
  • Notify users of changes through in-app notifications

6. Orders, Cancellation, Returns & Refunds

6.1 Cancellation Policy

  • Orders may be cancelled only before packaging begins
  • Once an order enters the "Packaging" or "Out for Delivery" status, cancellation is not permitted
  • Cancellation requests must be made through the app or by contacting customer support
  • Repeated cancellations may result in account restrictions

6.2 Returns & Refunds

No General Returns: Due to the perishable nature of our products, we do not accept general returns or offer routine refunds.

Exceptions: Refunds or replacements will be considered exclusively for:

  • Damaged or spoiled items upon delivery
  • Incorrect items delivered (wrong product or quantity)
  • Missing items from your order
  • Quality issues with products (e.g., rotten vegetables, expired items)

Claim Process:

  • Report issues within 2 hours of delivery
  • Provide clear photographic evidence showing the issue
  • Include order number and item details
  • Claims are reviewed on a case-by-case basis, and decisions are at Urban Mart's sole discretion
  • Resolution may include refund, replacement, or store credit

8. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information, including:

  • Encryption of data in transit using SSL/TLS protocols
  • Secure authentication mechanisms and phone-based OTP verification
  • Regular security assessments and vulnerability monitoring
  • Access controls limiting employee access to personal data
  • Secure hosting infrastructure with reputable cloud service providers
User Responsibility: No security system is completely infallible. Please keep your device secure, use strong authentication methods, never share OTP codes, and report any suspicious activity immediately.

9. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for different periods depending on the data type and purpose:

  • Account Data: Retained while your account is active and for up to 90 days after account deletion
  • Transaction Records: Retained for 7 years or as required by applicable tax and financial regulations
  • Communication Logs: Retained for up to 2 years for customer service and dispute resolution
  • Analytics Data: Aggregated and anonymized data may be retained indefinitely
  • Legal Hold: Data subject to legal proceedings or investigations retained until matter resolution

10. Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the following rights:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal information we hold about you
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and personal data (subject to legal retention requirements)
  • Portability: Request your data in a machine-readable format
  • Objection: Object to processing based on legitimate interests
  • Withdrawal of Consent: Withdraw previously granted consent (where processing is based on consent)
  • Opt-out: Unsubscribe from marketing communications (transactional messages cannot be opted out)

To exercise these rights, contact us using the information provided in Section 16.

11. Account Deletion

Deletion Process:

  • Initiate deletion request through in-app settings or by contacting support
  • Verify your identity through phone number confirmation
  • Account and associated personal data deleted within 7 business days
  • Transaction records retained as required by law (typically 7 years)
  • Anonymized data may be retained for analytics purposes

Consequences of Deletion: You will lose access to order history, saved addresses, preferences, and any pending orders will be cancelled.

12. Children's Privacy

Urban Mart is not intended for individuals under 13 years of age (or the applicable age of consent in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we discover that we have inadvertently collected information from a child under 13, we will:

  • Delete such information immediately
  • Terminate the associated account
  • Notify the parent or guardian if contact information is available

If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.
