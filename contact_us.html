Frequently Asked Questions

How do I place an order?

Browse our wide selection of fresh products in the app, add your desired items to the cart, select a convenient delivery time slot, and confirm your order. We'll deliver fresh products right to your doorstep at your chosen time.

Can I cancel or return an order?

Orders can be canceled only before the packing process begins. For your protection, returns or refunds are accepted exclusively for damaged, defective, or spoiled products. Please inspect your delivery upon arrival and contact us immediately if there are any quality concerns.

How do I reset my password or access my account?

Use the mobile OTP (One-Time Password) login option available in the app for secure, password-free access. If you experience any login difficulties, our support team is ready to assist you via phone or email.

Where do you deliver?

We currently serve Guntur, Andhra Pradesh with fresh vegetables, groceries, batters, and daily essentials. We use location verification to confirm service availability in your area. If you're within our delivery zone, you'll see available delivery slots when placing your order.

How is my personal data used and protected?

Your privacy is our priority. We use your phone number for login and delivery coordination, location data to verify service availability in your area, and optional information (name, email) to enhance your experience. All data is securely stored and encrypted, and can be deleted upon request. We never share your personal information with third parties.

How do I delete my account?

You can delete your account directly through the Urban Mart app in just a few steps. Go to your Profile, tap "Delete Account", verify your identity via OTP, and confirm. Your account will be deleted within 7 business days. For detailed instructions and alternative methods, visit our Account Deletion page.

What about refunds and returns?

Due to the perishable nature of our products, we don't accept general returns. However, we will gladly provide refunds or replacements for damaged, spoiled, incorrect, or missing items. Please report any issues within 2 hours of delivery with clear photos, and we'll review your case. Your satisfaction is important to us!

What payment methods do you accept?

We currently accept Cash on Delivery (COD) only. Online payment options will be introduced in future updates. When available, we'll use secure, PCI-DSS compliant payment gateways to protect your financial information.
